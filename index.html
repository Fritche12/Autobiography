About me

Hi there! Welcome to my autobiography website! I am so excited to share my journey with you. I created this website because I truly believe in the power of self-expression and connecting with others. My name is Ma. Fritche T. Zarandin and I am currently a second-year student at Romblon State University.

I am grateful for my loving and supportive family, who have taught me valuable life lessons. In my free time, I enjoy exploring new foods, watching movies, and browsing social media. A good friend is someone who is trustworthy, and you can rely on them when you need them. They listen without judging, and their presence is comforting in both good and bad times. Kindness is another valuable trait that a friend should possess. They show empathy, compassion, and genuine care for your well-being. They are considerate of your feelings and needs, and always offer a helping hand when required. Having friends from elementary to college is a remarkable experience that creates lifelong bonds and treasured memories. From elementary school, where you meet classmates and build a foundation, to middle and high school, where friendships expand through shared experiences, to college, where you meet people from diverse backgrounds, these friendships accompany you through different stages of life. These friends have supported you, celebrated your successes, and been there during challenging times. Cherish these friendships, as they are truly special and can withstand the test of time and distance.

Reliability is another positive characteristic that fosters trust and dependability. A reliable friend is someone you can count on, knowing that they will be there when you need them. They follow through on their commitments, and you can always rely on them to prioritize our friendships. Having friends with these positive characteristics enhances our lives, providing love, support, and shared memories.
